Welcome to CAS Lab

The Computational Aerospace Sciences Laboratory at the Indian Institute of Science focuses on cutting-edge research in computational fluid dynamics, aerospace engineering, and advanced numerical methods. Our interdisciplinary team works on solving complex problems in aerodynamics, propulsion, and space technology.

Research Areas

Computational Fluid Dynamics

Advanced numerical methods for solving complex flow problems in aerospace applications.

Turbulence Modeling

Development of novel turbulence models for high-speed and hypersonic flows.

Propulsion Systems

Analysis and design optimization of rocket engines and air-breathing propulsion systems.

Current Projects

Hypersonic Vehicle Aerothermodynamics

Funding: DRDO, ₹50 lakhs

Duration: 2023-2026

Description: Development of high-fidelity computational tools for predicting aerothermal loads on hypersonic vehicles during atmospheric entry.

AI-Enhanced Turbulence Modeling

Funding: DST-SERB, ₹35 lakhs

Duration: 2022-2025

Description: Integration of machine learning techniques with traditional RANS turbulence models for improved accuracy in separated flow predictions.

Scramjet Combustor Optimization

Funding: ISRO, ₹75 lakhs

Duration: 2024-2027

Description: Multi-objective optimization of scramjet combustor geometry using surrogate-based optimization techniques coupled with high-fidelity CFD.

High-Performance Computing for Aerospace

Funding: MeitY, ₹40 lakhs

Duration: 2023-2025

Description: Development of scalable parallel algorithms for aerospace CFD applications on next-generation supercomputing architectures.
