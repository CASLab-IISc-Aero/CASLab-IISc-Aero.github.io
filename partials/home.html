Welcome to CAS Lab

We develop high-order Discontinuous Galerkin (DG) methods for fluid simulations, focusing on compressible and hypersonic flows.
Our work includes mesh adaptation, curved mesh generation, and aerodynamic shape optimization for high-fidelity 2D/3D simulations.

Research Areas

High-Order Simulation

We build robust DG solvers for RANS and LES, enabling high-accuracy flow simulations.

Mesh Adaptation

Our algorithms generate adaptive anisotropic meshes for accurate, automated CFD simulations.

Shape Optimization

We apply mesh adaptation to improve airfoil and geometry design through fast, precise simulations.
